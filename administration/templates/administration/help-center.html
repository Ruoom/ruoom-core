{% blocktrans %}Your business information, such as your business name, URL, and the name & email of your account, will be set up based on your form inputs from the beta site sign-up form.

To set up your first business location information, please follow these steps:

1) First, visit the "RoomO" page by clicking on the left side menu. When the page loads, select Add Location. Then, fill out the fields in the form.

2) If you have customer data you need to import, visit the "Customers" page on the left side menu. From there, click on the Import Customer Data feature button. For more guidance, see the How to import customer data guide below.

3) Visit "Platform Settings" to configure additional business information and third-party integrations (KakaoTalk, WhatsApp, email client, and payment processors). Those with administrator privileges can access Platform Settings by clicking their username in the bottom-left corner.

If you're having trouble setting up any of your data, do not hesitate to reach out to our support team at support@ruoomsoftware.com.
